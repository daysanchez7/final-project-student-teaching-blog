Day's Student Teaching Blog

The team delivering my application will be a webapplication!

Description of Application Through the Lens of the User

The application Day’s Student-Teaching Blog will consist of multiple tabs. When the website is first open, the user will see a brief background of Dayanna Sanchez. Within the homepage, there are three tabs. The first tab will be where student-teachers can rate how they're feeling for their student-teaching. Depending how they feel, they will recieve a quote. The second tab will contain additional quotes to motivate student-teachers during their student-teaching. The third tab contains advice from Day on what to do and not to do during their student-teaching. Overall, Day’s Student-Teaching Blog is a community of student-teachers that Day will be encouraging.
