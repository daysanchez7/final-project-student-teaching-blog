Day's Student Teaching Blog

The team delivering my application will be a webapplication!

Description of Application Through the Lens of the User

The application Day’s Student-Teaching Blog will consist of multiple tabs. When the website is first open, the user will see a brief background of Dayanna Sanchez. Within the homepage, there are three tabs. The first tab will contain daily quotes given by Day to motivate student-teachers during their student-teaching. The second tab contains advice from Day on what to do and not to do during their student-teaching. The third tab will be where other student-teachers can post any advice they want to give. Overall, Day’s Student-Teaching Blog is a community of student-teachers that are going to encourage each other.
